# ⚡ Reliability & Performance Engineering

## 🎯 Objective
Build systems that survive failures and respond instantly.

## 🛡️ Resilience Patterns
- **Circuit Breaker:** Prevent cascading failures.
- **Bulkheading:** Isolate resources to contain damage.
- **Retry Logic:** Exponential backoff with jitter.
- **Rate Limiting:** Protect against DDoS and resource exhaustion.

## 🚄 Performance
- **Caching:** CDN, Redis (distributed cache), In-memory.
- **Profiling:** Identify bottlenecks in CPU/Memory.
- **Observability:** Metrics (Prometheus), Logging (ELK), Tracing (Jaeger).
